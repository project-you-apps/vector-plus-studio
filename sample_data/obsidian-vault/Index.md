# Index — Renaissance Astronomy Vault

A small synthetic vault used to test the Cart Builder's Obsidian source-type
adapter. Topics center on the Copernican revolution: heliocentrism,
telescope-era observations, and the scientists whose work converged into
modern astronomy.

## People

- [[Galileo Galilei]] — telescope, Jovian moons, heliocentrism advocate
- [[Johannes Kepler]] — laws of planetary motion, optics, mysticism
- [[Tycho Brahe]] — most precise pre-telescope observations on record

## Concepts

- [[Heliocentrism]] — sun-centered model
- [[Geocentrism]] — earth-centered model (the prior consensus)
- [[Telescope]] — the instrument that broke the old model
